**English**
```
Bodacare is your everyday health companion — for the meds you take, the numbers you track, and the lab results you don't quite understand.

— Key features

• Medication reminders — Add your prescriptions and dosing schedule, get reliable push notifications. Snap a photo of your prescription to fill in details automatically.

• Vitals tracking — Log blood pressure, glucose, weight, and body composition (InBody) and watch trends form into graphs over time. InBody result sheets can be scanned with a single photo.

• Lab result OCR — Photograph your lab sheet and get a clean table of every test and value. Ask the AI questions about your results in plain language.

• AI nutrition coach — Photograph a meal and get calories, protein, carbs, and fat. Set a goal (lose, gain, maintain) and get personalized daily targets and coaching.

• Health reports — A periodic AI summary of your medication, vitals, body composition, and nutrition patterns. Ask follow-up questions about any report.

• Care circle — Connect family members so they can see your health overview and gently nudge you when you miss a dose.

— Your privacy

• Bodacare staff never read or analyze your health data.
• Your data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising or marketing.
• Photos are not stored. Prescription, lab, and meal photos are discarded immediately after AI analysis; only the extracted text and numbers are kept.
• Delete your account anytime to remove all your data instantly.

— Important medical disclaimer

Bodacare is a self-care companion, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. AI output is informational only — always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions. For emergencies, call your local emergency number.

Contact: admin@bodacare.com
Website: https://bodacare.com
```

## Age Rating

Health & Fitness apps that include medication tracking and unfiltered medical info typically rate **12+**. AI features that discuss medical results may push to **17+**. Apple's questionnaire will calculate; expect 12+ or 17+.

When filling the rating questionnaire:
- Medical/Treatment Information → **Infrequent/Mild** (informational, with disclaimers, not diagnostic)
- All other categories → None

### Notes for Apple Reviewer

```
Bodacare is a Korean-market personal health companion. It tracks medications, blood pressure, glucose, weight, body composition (InBody), and lab results, and provides AI-assisted nutrition coaching and lab-result interpretation.

A few notes that may help review:

1. PHONE-BASED SIGNUP. The app uses phone number + password (Korean format 010-XXXX-XXXX) instead of email. SMS verification is intentionally not used — the phone number is used as an account identifier only. A demo account is provided above.

2. AI FEATURES use Google Gemini via our backend. Photos taken inside the app (prescriptions, lab sheets, meal photos, InBody sheets) are sent to Gemini for OCR/analysis and ARE NOT stored on our servers. Only the extracted text/numbers are retained. This is disclosed in the in-app privacy policy and sensitive-information consent screen.

3. MEDICAL DISCLAIMER. The app is informational only. It does not diagnose or treat. The Terms of Service and sensitive-info consent screens carry explicit medical disclaimers and direct users to contact qualified healthcare professionals.

4. ACCOUNT DELETION. Users can delete their account end-to-end from Profile → "계정 삭제" (Delete Account). This removes their record and all linked health data immediately.

5. PUSH NOTIFICATIONS. The app schedules local notifications for medication reminders (on-device, no server roundtrip) and also sends a server-side push reminder via Expo Push Service if a dose is missed. Both require user consent at first launch.

6. CARE CIRCLE. Users can voluntarily share their health data with a family member ("보호자"). The connection requires explicit acceptance on both sides; data is never shared with anyone the user has not connected to.

7. LANGUAGE. Primary language is Korean. English UI is available via Profile → Language.

Contact for review questions: admin@bodacare.com
```
